# Sources & attribution

OSINT Resources Hub stands on the work of the community. We keep tool **URLs**
(which are facts) and add our own taxonomy, vetting, and health checks — we don't
republish any collection's own structure. This page credits the collections and
maintainers whose work helped surface the tools we list.

If you maintain a listed resource and want your attribution corrected or an entry
removed, open an issue with the `takedown` label or email `info@osintresourceshub.com`.

## How we treat each source

- **Facts, not structure.** We record a tool's URL and attach our own category,
  tags, and provenance. We do not copy a source's card layout, folder names, or
  descriptions.
- **Terms-aware.** Before drawing on any source we check its licence/terms; where a
  source's terms don't permit reuse, we link out to it rather than incorporate it.
- **Attribution as a signal.** When several independent collections list the same
  tool, that raises its trust score here — overlap makes the catalog stronger, not
  more bloated.
